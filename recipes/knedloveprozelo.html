Knedlo Vepřo Zelo



Description: Knedlo vepřo zelo is traditional Czech food that you shuld serve with glass of beer and watch football when you eat it


Ingredients:

  • 500g of pork meat
  • 5 pieces of garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 50g of pork lard
  • cabbage
  • 70g of bacon

Setup:

  1. Setup oven temperature to 170 °C.
  2. Mix meat with salt, pepper and garlic
  3. Make the meat in the oven until its brittle, mix it with warm water
  4. add cabagge with bacon and cook it for another 15 minutes
